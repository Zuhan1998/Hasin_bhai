What we do

Cryptic Security organization supports Bangladesh's cyber policy, improves the reliability of ICT goods, services, and procedures through cybersecurity certification programs, collaborates with public and private organizations, and aids Bangladeshi government organizations in preparing for future cyber challenges. The organization collaborates with its key stakeholders to strengthen trust in the connected economy, increase the resilience of Bangladesh's digital infrastructure, and ultimately maintain the security of Bangladesh's society and citizens online through knowledge sharing, capacity building, and awareness raising.
